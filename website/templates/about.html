About Our Camp

Our hockey camp was established in 2000 with the mission to promote the sport of hockey and foster a community of passionate players and fans. Over the years, we have grown into one of the most respected hockey camps in the region, welcoming players of all skill levels.

Camp History

Since our inception, we have hosted over 10,000 players at our camps. Each year, our camp continues to evolve, offering new and exciting activities for our participants.

In our early years, we focused on basic skills training, but as our reputation grew, we expanded our programs to include advanced techniques, off-ice conditioning, and video analysis sessions.

Over the past decade, we have introduced team-building activities, guest speakers from the professional hockey world, and specialized training sessions led by former NHL players.

Our camp has become a staple in the community, providing young athletes with the tools and inspiration they need to excel in hockey and beyond. We are proud to have a lasting impact on our participants, many of whom return year after year.
